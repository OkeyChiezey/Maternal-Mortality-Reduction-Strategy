üìä Results & Recommendations
Key Findings:

The ANC Lifeline: States with lower Antenatal Care attendance experience disproportionately higher maternal deaths.


Priority States: Katsina, Jigawa, Bauchi, Zamfara, and Kebbi are identified as the most immediate focus areas for intensified programming.

Strategic Recommendations:

Resource Allocation: Allocate a disproportionately higher share of funding and supplies (e.g., oxytocin, magnesium sulfate) to the top 5 high-burden states.



Infrastructure Upgrades: Rehabilitate Primary Healthcare Centers (PHCs) specifically in rural underserved areas.



Community Integration: Train and integrate Traditional Birth Attendants (TBAs) as referral agents rather than primary attendants to bridge cultural gaps.
